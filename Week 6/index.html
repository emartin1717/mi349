Banana Smoothie

Serves Two

Ingredient's Required:

  • Two Bananas
  • 1/2 Cup Vanilla Yogurt and Milk
  • Two Teaspoons Honey
  • Pinch of Cinnamon
  • One Cup of Ice

Prep Time:

  • Approximately five minutes. Blend until smooth.

Triple-Berry Smoothie

Serves Two

Ingredient's Required:

  • 1 and 1/2 Cups mixed: Blackberries, Strawberries, Raspberries
  • One Cup Milk
  • One Cup Ice
  • Sugar to Taste

Prep Time:

  • Approximately five minutes. Blend until smooth.

Tangerine Smoothie

Serves Two

Ingredient's Required:

  • Four Peeled and Seeded Tangerines
  • Juice of Two Limes
  • 1/4 Cup Honey
  • One Cup Ice

Prep Time:

  • Approximately five minutes. Blend until smooth.
